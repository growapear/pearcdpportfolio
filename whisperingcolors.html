Whispering Colors

an interactive exploration of color, gesture, and storytelling through real-time interaction and machine learning.

RoleInteractive Artist, Developer
Timeline 3 Months
Year 2024
Project MADE Brown-Risd Project

The concept behind "Whispering Colors" is to create a living canvas where light is not only seen but felt and controlled. This installation transforms light into an interactive element that responds to and evolves with viewer participation. Itâ€™s an experiment in giving form to the intangible, allowing participants to 'sculpt' light through their movements, creating a deeply personal and ever-changing art experience.

The project lies in my fascination with the transformational power of light and its profound impact on human perception. This project is inspired by James Turrell's mastery of sculpting space and emotion through illumination, translating these elements into a digital and interactive medium. My goal is to bridge the gap between physical light installations and digital interactivity, creating a platform where art becomes a dialogue between the creator, the viewer, and the medium itself.

This project allows me to explore another layer of my career as a designer. It is an opportunity for me to practice using new tools to hone my technical skills with my philosophical inquiries into the nature of interaction and perception. It challenges me to apply and extend my understanding of design and technology in creating an immersive, responsive environment that not only entertains but also engages on an emotional and cognitive level.

I aim to evoke a sense of agency and creativity in participants, encouraging them to engage actively with the installation. The desired emotional response is multifacetedâ€”ranging from tranquility and meditative calm as viewers observe the gentle flow of colors, to exhilaration and empowerment as they realize their movements can shape the visual output. Ultimately, the installation should leave participants with a renewed appreciation for the playful and powerful role of light in shaping our perceived reality.

Presenting this project in a large-scale, interactive digital mural where movements directly translate into visual changes. I want to emphasize the impact of light that allows the interactive experience to be accessible and engaging for a broad audience.

For me, this project is more than an assignment. It is a platform for exploring digital interactivity as a form of expressive, personal, and communal art. It redefines the art space as a collaborative, transformative environment. This project is crucial for my development as a designer who bridges the digital with the tangible, pushing the boundaries of how art is created and experienced.
